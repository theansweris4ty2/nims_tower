<h1 style = "color: purple">Tower of Nim</h1>
<p style = "font-style: italic">The Wizard Nim has been betrayed by his traitorous apprentice Zachariah. While you Nim's trusted familiar were thrown into the dugeons of the tower. You must make your way to the top of the tower to save Nim, but along the way you be forced to confront a multitude of vile creatures created through Zachariah's diabolical experiments.
 </p>

 <p style = "font-style: italic">You will all of your wits, stealth and guile to navigate your way up the tower. Each floor will present a new challenger for you to overcome. Use your knowledge of magic and herbology learned through many years of assisting Nim to create potions that will grant abilities you can use to defeat foes, pass obstacles, and defeat the evil Zachariah </p>

 <h3>If you fail Nim and and the kingdom will be lost!
